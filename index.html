Quick Reference

Quick ways to recycle in Algiers

Paper/cardboard can be given to paper collectors or Ecopap drives. PET bottles and HDPE containers can be handed to local plastic collectors. Metal cans can be taken to scrapyards. Donate clothes and books to local associations. Electronics can be passed to repair shops. Compost food scraps if you have a balcony or yard.

## Design System: Minimalist Y2K

A clean, functional design inspired by early-2000s interfaces. Nostalgic without looking like it crawled out of a dusty CRT monitor.

*   **Color Palette**
    *   **Background:** White or very light gray (#F0F0F0)
    *   **Primary Accent:** Hyperlink Blue (#0000FF)
    *   **Secondary Accent:** Silver / Soft Chrome (#C0C0C0)
    *   **Success State:** Neon Green (#39FF14 or similar)

*   **Typography**
    *   **Headers:** Pixel-style or Monospace fonts (ex: Courier New, Perfect DOS, Pixel Operator)
    *   **Body Text:** Clean system fonts (Verdana, Tahoma, Arial)

*   **UI Elements**
    *   **Buttons:**
        *   1px solid borders
        *   Subtle bevel or drop-shadow for a flattened Win95/Win98 feel
        *   Slight color inversion or underline on hover
    *   **Containers / Panels:**
        *   Visible borders
        *   Optional “window title bar” styling at the top for nostalgia
        *   Light padding to keep things airy, not cramped

*   **Interactions**
    *   **Hover effects that:**
        *   Invert foreground/background, or
        *   Underline blue text
    *   Simple transitions, nothing flashy
